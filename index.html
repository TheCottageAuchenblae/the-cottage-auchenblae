Welcome

A peaceful, pet-friendly escape in the heart of Aberdeenshire. The Cottage Auchenblae offers a cosy getaway for couples and their four-legged friends. With stunning walks, local distilleries, and beautiful coastlines nearby, it's the perfect base to explore Scotland. When booking direct, up to 2 dogs can stay for free. Send a booking request and we'll confirm your dates and send you a Stripe payment link, or ask you to make the payment through the website, you'll then have 24 hours to make the payment. Bookings Friday, Saturday, Sunday are 2 night minimum / Monday - Thursday 1 Night bookings available.
